About Me

Jim is head of developer advocacy at Pieces, focusing on enabling developers to be more productive by leveraging contextual awareness of not only the code they write, but the content the read and the conversations they have. He’s British, so sounds way smarter than he actually is, and lives in the Pacific North West of the USA. In the past he’s lived in 4 continents working as a developer in the mobile, desktop, and scientific space. He’s spoken at conferences and events all around the globe, organised meetup groups and communities, and written books on mobile development and IoT. He’s also a Microsoft MVP for AI and Developer Tools. Here are a few technologies I've been working with recently:
